About ProdSoft

ProdSoft QR-Utility is a QR code scanner. It can scan QR codes from images and through any webcam. Use it online without downloading any app.

The ProdSoft QR web app scans QR codes locally without uploading them to our servers. Your data and privacy are our top priority. Built with Cosmo Wolfe's JavaScript port of Google's ZXing library.
